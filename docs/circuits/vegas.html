About

This track welcomes the glitz and glamour of the entertainment capital, the Las Vegas Street Circuit offers a thrilling urban racing experience against the backdrop of the city's iconic landmarks. This newly introduced track is characterized by long, fast straights that wind through the bustling streets, providing adrenaline-pumping moments for drivers and fans alike.
