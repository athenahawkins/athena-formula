About

AlphaTauri, formerly known as Toro Rosso, has carved its niche as an integral part of Formula 1's landscape. As the sister team to Red Bull Racing, it plays a crucial role in the Red Bull development track, showcasing a fusion of ambition, technological innovation, and nurturing emerging talent. Evolving from its origins, the team stands as a testament to perseverance, adaptability, and a relentless pursuit of success on the track. With a history rooted in the sport's competitive spirit, AlphaTauri embodies a commitment to fostering a legacy of spirited racing, cementing its position as a pivotal entity in the Formula 1 fraternity.
