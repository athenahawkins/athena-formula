About

Alpine, a French racing team in Formula One, has roots dating back to the foundation of the Alpine brand in the 1950s by Jean Rédélé. While initially focused on rallying and endurance racing, Alpine ventured into Formula One as a constructor and has a rich history intertwined with motorsport. The team, formerly known as Renault, transitioned to the Alpine name in 2021, marking a new chapter while maintaining the Renault Group's heritage. Alpine embodies a blend of French flair and technical expertise, aiming to leverage their racing pedigree and innovative spirit to thrive in the competitive world of Formula One.
