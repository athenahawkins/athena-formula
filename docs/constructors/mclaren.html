About

McLaren Racing, one of the most illustrious names in Formula One history, was established by New Zealand racer Bruce McLaren in the 1960s. Renowned for its relentless pursuit of excellence, McLaren has a rich legacy in F1, boasting multiple Constructors' and Drivers' Championships. The team has seen legendary drivers like Ayrton Senna, Alain Prost, and Lewis Hamilton contribute to its success. Despite facing transitional phases in recent years, McLaren's resilience, technological innovation, and passionate fanbase continue to propel them forward, symbolizing a commitment to pushing boundaries in motorsport.
