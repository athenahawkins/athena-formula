About

The Mercedes-AMG Petronas Formula One Team stands as an emblem of motorsport excellence, etching its name in the annals of Formula 1 history. Renowned for their dominance in the 2010s, securing multiple Constructors' Championships, Mercedes' legacy is defined by an unwavering commitment to precision engineering and performance. Led by visionary minds, their pursuit of innovation and speed has propelled them to the forefront of the sport.
