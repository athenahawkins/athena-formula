About

Carlos Sainz, a Spanish racing sensation, burst onto the Formula 1 scene, showcasing his talent in 2015. Following in the footsteps of his rallying champion father, Sainz carved his path with Toro Rosso before moving to Renault and then McLaren. His transition to Scuderia Ferrari in 2021 marked a new chapter, where his tenacity and consistency led to podium finishes and established him as a vital asset alongside Charles Leclerc.
