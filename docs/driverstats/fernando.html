About

Fernando Alonso, a Spanish racing icon, made his mark in Formula 1 in 2001, exhibiting his prodigious talent with Minardi before moving to Renault and then to McLaren. A double World Champion, Alonso's legacy is defined by his sheer determination and tactical prowess on the track. His return to Formula 1 with Alpine in 2021 marked a triumphant comeback, showcasing his enduring passion and skill, solidifying his status as one of the sport's all-time greats.
