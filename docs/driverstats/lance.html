About

Lance Stroll, a Canadian driver, debuted in Formula 1 in 2017, showcasing promise and skill with Williams before joining Racing Point, now known as Aston Martin. Notably, Stroll's connection extends beyond the cockpit as his father, Lawrence Stroll, holds a prominent role in the leadership of Aston Martin. Lance's journey in Formula 1 reflects his growth and adaptability on the grid. His tenure at Aston Martin represents a significant chapter in his career, where his consistent performances and alignment with the team's vision, guided in part by his father's leadership, contribute to the team's ambitions in Formula 1.
