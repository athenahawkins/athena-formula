About

Valtteri Bottas, a Finnish driver, entered Formula 1 in 2013, showcasing his talents and racing prowess with Williams before joining Mercedes, a team he competed with for several successful seasons. Bottas, known for his consistency and teamwork, contributed significantly to Mercedes' dominance during his tenure. His move to Alfa Romeo Racing ORLEN marks a new chapter in his career, where his experience and dedication continue to make an impact. Bottas' valuable contributions on and off the track position him as a key driver for Alfa Romeo Racing ORLEN, as the team aims for progression and success in Formula 1.
