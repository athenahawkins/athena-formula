About

Zhou Guanyu, a Chinese driver, made his mark in Formula 1 in 2022, debuting with Alfa Romeo Racing ORLEN. His entry into Formula 1 marked a significant moment as the first full-time Chinese driver in the sport's history. Zhou's arrival brought forth a wave of excitement and anticipation, showcasing his talent and potential. His promising performances and dedication on the track have positioned him as a key asset for Alfa Romeo Racing ORLEN, marking a new era in Chinese representation within Formula 1 and igniting hopes for a successful future in the sport.
