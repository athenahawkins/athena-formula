Sainz devastated after 10-place grid penalty in Vegas

Nov. 17, 2023

By Athena Hawkins
Formula1 Females

The Las Vegas Grand Prix witnessed an unexpected turn for Carlos Sainz as a consequence of the track-related damage. Following the compromised track conditions necessitating an engine replacement, Sainz incurred a ten-place grid penalty, relegating him to a challenging starting position for the forthcoming race.

The incident stemmed from the track's compromised integrity, prompting Sainz's engine swap due to the incurred damage. This setback undoubtedly poses a formidable challenge for the driver, positioning him further down the starting order for the subsequent race.

As Formula 1 continues to navigate unforeseen track challenges, Sainz's situation stands as a testament to the sport's inherent unpredictability. His task ahead involves a strategic and skillful maneuver through the grid to optimize his race outcome in the upcoming event.
